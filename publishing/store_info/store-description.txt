See your portfolio positions directly on investing.com asset pages!

When you browse stocks, ETFs, or other assets on investing.com, this extension displays your holdings right on the page - no need to switch back and forth to your portfolio.

FEATURES:

• Position Panel - Shows your quantity, average buy price, total value, and P/L directly on the asset page
• Real-Time Updates - Total value and P/L update automatically as the price changes on the page
• Multi-Portfolio Support - Automatically aggregates holdings across all your investing.com portfolios
• Privacy Focused - All data stays in your browser. No external servers, no tracking, no analytics

HOW IT WORKS:

1. Log in to your investing.com account
2. Visit any stock or ETF page
3. If that asset is in one of your holdings portfolios, your position details appear automatically

WHAT YOU'LL SEE:

• Quantity - Number of shares you own
• Avg. Buy Price - Your cost basis
• Total Value - Current market value (updates in real-time)
• P/L - Profit/loss in dollars and percentage (updates in real-time)

REQUIREMENTS:

• An investing.com account with portfolio holdings
• Must be logged in to investing.com for the extension to work

PRIVACY:

This extension does NOT collect any personal data. Your portfolio information is fetched directly from investing.com and stored locally in your browser for caching purposes only. No data is ever sent to external servers.

OPEN SOURCE:

This extension is open source. View the code and report issues on GitHub.
